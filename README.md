Kepler's Harmony of the World
=============================

Johannes Kepler's 1619 work of astronomy resolutionized science, giving us what we now refer to as Kepler's Laws. Using Tycho Brahe's observations, he proved that the planets move in elliptical orbits around the Sun, rather then perfect circles as Galileo and everyone else believed. 

But what most people don't know is that much of the work was concerned with music. Kepler found that the minumum and maximum angular speeds corresponded to harmonic proportions.

This project sets out to play the music of the Spheres that Kepler discovered.
